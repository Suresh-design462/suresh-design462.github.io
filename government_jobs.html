MPPSC District Public Health Nurse Officer Jobs 2025 Details

Recruiting Authority

Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission.

Post Name & Vacancy

District Public Health Nurse Officer- 12 (UR-03, SC-01, ST-01, OBC-04, EWS-01).

Appointment Nature

Recruited individuals will be posted under Department of Public Health & Medical Education on regular basis on time scale of pay. Probation will be of Two years as per MP Government norms.

Salary

Recruited individuals will receive salary as per pay scale Rs.15600-Rs.39100+Grade Pay Rs.5400. Other applicable allowances will be paid as per existing government norms.

MPPSC DPHNO Application Dates

  • Starting Date to Apply: 25-09-2025
  • Last Date to Apply: 24-10-2025
  • Starting Date for Correction of Applications: 01-10-2025
  • Last Date for Correction of Application Forms: 26-10-2025
  • Last Date for Sending Hard Copies to Commission: 31-10-2025

MPPSC Public Health Nurse Officer Eligibility 2025

Age Limit

Upper age limit is 40 years. However age relaxation is apply as per MP Govt norms. SC/ST/OBC- 05 years.

Educational Qualification

  1. Individuals should have completed B.Sc Nursing or M.Sc Nursing and
  2. Post Graduate Degree/Post Graduate Diploma in Public Health Management from regular course.
  3. Registration in State Nursing Council & Employment Office is must.

Experience Required

10 years of regular service as Nursing Officer/Senior Nursing Officer/Sister Tutor/Nursing Superintendent Grade 2 is necessary.

MPPSC PHNO Selection Process

Selection will be through Interview. Date of Interview will be intimated later.

MPPSC District PHNO Application Process

Individuals meeting all eligibility criteria may submit online applications in given period. After submission, printout of application forms along with required documents as per appendices given in notification should be submitted to official address by registered post/speed post.

Application fee of Rs.500 for EWS/UR and Rs.250 for SC/ST/OBC candidates should be paid.

Application forms once submitted can be corrected in given time window with correction fee of Rs.50.

Address to send Hard Copies

Secretary, Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission, Indore, Madhya Pradesh- 452001
