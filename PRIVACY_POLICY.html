1. Information We Collect

Our core philosophy is to collect as little information as possible.

Information We DO NOT Collect

We do not collect any personal information that can identify you, such as your:

  • Name, email, or phone number

You are **not required to create an account** to use InstaNurse.

Information Collected by Our Advertising Partner

To keep InstaNurse completely free, we partner with a third-party advertising service. This partner collects **non-personal information** to show you relevant ads. This information may include:

  • Device Identifiers: Such as your device's Advertising ID.
  • IP Address: Used to determine a general, non-specific location.
  • Device Information: Like your device type, operating system, and network.

This data is used by our partner to serve ads and is governed by their own privacy policy. We **do not have direct access to this information.**

2. How We Use Information

The only purpose of the data collected by our partner is to:

  • Support the App: By displaying ads, we can keep InstaNurse free for all users.
  • Provide Relevant Ads: This helps ensure you see ads that are interesting to you and are not repetitive.

Job Portal Disclaimer

The InstaNurse Job Portal is a **job aggregation service, not a recruitment agency.** We simply list job opportunities that have been officially published by healthcare organizations on their own websites. We do not announce or offer any jobs ourselves.
